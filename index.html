About Me

I'm an Ottawa based Designer with a passion for art. I keep a close eye on details and always strive to push my work to the next level. I love indulging myself with all types of work from marketing, layouts, reports to even UI/UX design. There is a thrill I get when you're able to show someone a design when you first started and it is a such a narrow concept, to the final piece where they may not even know how you did it. Currently I am finishing my last year at Algonquin College for Graphic Design. After that I am planning to make a mark on the industry and find my place. So if you would like to work with me in the future; or simple share a bottle and talk over design just shoot me an email and we'll work something out.
